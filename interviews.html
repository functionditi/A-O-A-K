INTERVIEWS



Vijaya Mohan

Rangoli Artist

Vijaya Mohan an Indian Singaporean is a renowned and acclaimed Rangoli artiste (Indian courtyard painting and floor art). She holds the Guinness World Record for the largest Rangoli in the World and additionally holds 29 Singapore records in various categories ( the largest, longest, most number of people etc).
